About me

Hey there! My name is Todd Morrill.

In August, 2015, I joined a team of data scientists in an AI innovation lab at PwC. Our mandate is to explore the technical and commercial feasibility of new AI and machine learning tools and techniques. I'm still not sure why they hired me but the hard work has paid off and I feel less and less imposter syndrome everyday.

Over the past 4 years I jumped into math, statistics, and computer science. Outside of my day job building deep learning models for clients I have been fortunate enough to taking some rewarding classes, namely: Multivariable Calculus, Linear Algebra, Probability, Data Structures & Algorithms, and Systems Programming and Machine Organization. I can't say enough positive things about Harvard Extension School. These are the undergraduate classes are classes currently taught at Harvard so it's been fun to be a part of that.

I currently live in New York City. After finishing up a couple more classes, I hope to pursue a masters degree or PhD program (if they'll have me!!). All views expressed on this site are my own.
